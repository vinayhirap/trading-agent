Walk-Forward Theory

Why walk-forward? Static train/test splits are misleading for time-series data — future data leaks into training. Walk-forward prevents this.

7-fold structure: Each fold trains on previous N months, tests on next M months. Accuracy across folds gives honest out-of-sample performance.

Triple Barrier labels: Instead of fixed lookahead, labels are determined by which barrier (SL/target/time) is hit first — giving realistic trade outcomes.

EV framing: 52% accuracy at 1:1.5 R:R → EV = +0.025 per trade. Profitable even without high accuracy.
